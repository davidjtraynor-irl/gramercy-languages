About Gramercy Languages

We know what
these schools
expect.

At New York City's most demanding independent schools, a language course is never just a language course. The standards are high, the assessments are rigorous, and the cumulative record a student builds through middle and high school carries real weight. It matters in the classroom, with their teachers, and ultimately in the college admissions process.

Gramercy Languages was founded by educators who spent decades inside these schools. We understand the curricula, the expectations, and the pressure students are under. Our role is to ensure that language is never the thing that holds a student back; for the students who are ready, it becomes one of the things that sets them apart.

  • Deep familiarity with NYC independent school curricula and assessment standards
  • Specialized focus on language learning only; no generalists
  • Instruction calibrated to each student's school, year group, and goals
  • Focused on language mastery beyond standard examination preparation
  • Flexible in-person and online options around demanding school schedules

Our approach

Language is more than grammar.
It is connection.

Gramercy creates a supportive and engaging environment where students feel comfortable taking risks, speaking confidently, and developing a genuine connection with the language and its culture.

Real communication, not memorisation

We focus on authentic conversation and expression, using culture, current events, music, film, and real-world materials to bring the language to life.

Confidence over anxiety

Our approach is designed to reduce the fear of speaking and build the kind of genuine fluency that comes from feeling comfortable, not just prepared. Emphasis on natural pronunciation from the start.

Engaged families, visible progress

We use interactive, multimedia-rich lessons and maintain clear communication with both students and families so progress is always visible and goals stay on track.
